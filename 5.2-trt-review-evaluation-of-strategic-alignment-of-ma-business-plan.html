2- Evaluation of Strategic Alignment of MA Business Plan
Strategic Pillar Key Focus Area Questions
Center Care on People Abortion Care Does the Business Plan (BP) seek to expand access to abortion care through its services or its advocacy? If so, does it seek to diversify service delivery models (by providing or facilitating abortions through self-care, telehealth, and other person-centred strategies such as peer provision and home visits)?
Fertility Care Does the BP address issues of Fertility through its care model?
HIV Integration Does the BP look to optimize MA clinics and care platforms for HIV prevention, including the use of new technologies?
Marginalised Populations Does the BP credibly identify and propose strategies to engage with and reach marginalized people (particularly people excluded due to their sexual orientation or gender identity; people living with HIV, indigenous communities and people with disability)? Does the BP provide care and advocate for safe SRHR of adolescent and young people, including contraception, abortion and HIV/STIs?
Humanitarian Crisis If the MA is in a conflict or disaster-prone country, does the BP provide for expanding access to lifesaving SRHR care as well as advocate for the integration of SRHR into disaster management planning and policies?
Digital and Self-care Does the BP advance and document digital and self-care as part of a package of innovative service delivery models in a way that complements face-to-face delivery?
Move the Sexuality Agenda Harmful Laws and Norms Does the BP include credible efforts to increase domestic SRHR and gender equality financing , and to get CSE into curricula & education policies? Does it include work to prevent, address, respond to SGBV.
Youth Engagement and CSE Does the Business Plan engage with youth collectives and networks to expand access to SRHR and CSE? Does the BP budget allocate at least the equivalent of 5% of the core IPF to youth-led initiatives?
Solidarity for Change Partnerships Does the BP identify partners (and/or social movements) with whom to partner in order to advance SRHR?
Research Does the BP include any form of operational research and/or knowledge sharing?
Nurture our Federation Sustainability Does the BP outline a pathway to increase local revenues beyond aid funding (e.g. social enterprise, insurance systems to sustain service delivery, etc?)
Anti-discrimination and Inclusion Does the BP include efforts to ensure gender diversity, and anti-racism, anti-discrimination in policy and practise in the MAs workforce?
