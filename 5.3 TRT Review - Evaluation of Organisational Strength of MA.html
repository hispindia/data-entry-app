3 - Evaluation of Organizational Strength of MA

This form evaluates whether the MA has organizational capacity issues which might impact its ability to carry out its business plan effectively. Only Regional Staff members should fill out this sheet. TRT Members will consult this sheet to inform their decisions. Regional staff should only flag very significant issues. All organizations have areas for growth, and this form is not intended to cover all of those.

Please only complete relevant rows, and you can leave others blank (e.g., no need to complete rows on areas where MA does not have issues)

If flagging any issues, please include specific evidence or examples to back up your claim. Please also include specific steps the MA would need to take to remedy the issue.
